⚙️ Quick Start Guide

1. Enter Your Credentials

Add your SF Rec & Parks account username and password in the form above. Your credentials are stored securely in memory.

2. Start the Bot

Click "Start Bot" to begin automatic monitoring. The bot will check for Alice Marble court availability every 5 minutes.

3. Monitor Activity

Use "Try Now" to test immediately, or watch the Live Activity Log for automatic attempts. The bot will log all reservation attempts.

Note: This bot uses in-memory storage. Your data will be lost when the server restarts, but the bot will continue working as long as it's running.
